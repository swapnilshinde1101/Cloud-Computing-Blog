Understanding Cloud Computing Basics and Services

Learn the fundamentals of cloud computing and the key services provided by AWS, Azure, Google Cloud Platform, and more.

What is Cloud Computing?


Cloud computing refers to the delivery of computing services like servers, storage, databases, networking, software, and more over the internet. It allows businesses and individuals to access technology resources without owning physical infrastructure.
